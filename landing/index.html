Features

Built to keep you on track

Every feature exists for one reason: to shorten the distance between "I opened YouTube" and "I got what I needed, then left."

One-click Focus Mode

Hide related videos, comments, Shorts links, notifications and the recommendation grid instantly — toggle it back off whenever you actually want to browse.

Session time tracking

See exactly how long you've been watching in this session, right from the popup. Double-click to reset and start fresh.

Video count

A live counter of how many distinct videos you've watched, so the habit of "just one more video" stops hiding from you.

Channel whitelist

Only need YouTube for a few channels — a course, a client, a creator? Whitelist them and everything else stays out of view.

Timestamped notes

Drop a note at any point in a video, jump back to that exact moment later, and import/export your notes as JSON.

Private by design

No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Everything — timers, notes, whitelist — is stored locally in your browser.

How it works

Three steps to a quieter feed

1

Install the extension

Add Focus Mode to Chrome in a few seconds — no sign-up required.

2

3

Watch, then leave

With nothing to click into next, you finish your video and get back to your day.
